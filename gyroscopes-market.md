<p><p>The gyroscopes market is primarily divided into two types: sensing gyroscopes and indicating gyroscopes. Sensing gyroscopes are used for navigation and stabilization in various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and robotics, leveraging their ability to measure angular velocity. In contrast, indicating gyroscopes provide visual indications of orientation and tilt, often used in maritime and aviation instruments. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing accuracy and stability in motion detection and orientation systems across diverse industries.</p></p>
