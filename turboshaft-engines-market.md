<p><strong>The Turboshaft Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial-Flow Type</li><li>Centrifugal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboshaft engine market is primarily divided into two types: axial-flow and centrifugal types. Axial-flow turbine engines feature a design where air flows parallel to the shaft, offering high efficiency and performance, mainly used in helicopters and marine applications. In contrast, centrifugal-type engines utilize a radial flow design, compressing air outward to generate thrust, making them suitable for smaller UAVs and turboshaft applications in various industries. Both types cater to diverse performance requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
