<p><p>A Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT) is an innovative technology that utilizes optical fibers to measure electric current with high precision and reliability. It operates based on the principles of the Faraday effect, where the magnetic field surrounding a conductor influences the polarization state of light transmitted through the optical fiber. FOCTs offer several advantages over traditional current transformers, including immunity to electromagnetic interference, lightweight construction, and enhanced safety in high-voltage environments.</p><p>The Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period. Key trends influencing the market include the integration of fiber optic sensing technologies in renewable energy applications and a growing focus on reducing maintenance costs and enhancing system reliability. Additionally, advancements in optical fiber materials and manufacturing techniques are expected to further propel market expansion. The global trend towards digitization and the need for better energy management systems also contribute to the increasing traction of FOCTs in various industries, including utilities and transportation.</p></p>
