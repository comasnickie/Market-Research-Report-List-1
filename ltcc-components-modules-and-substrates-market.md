<p><p>The LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic) market consists of three primary segments: LTCC Components, LTCC Modules, and LTCC Substrates. LTCC Components include passive elements like capacitors and inductors integrated within the ceramic substrate. LTCC Modules are higher-level assemblies that combine components to perform specific functions, often used in RF and microwave applications. LTCC Substrates serve as the foundational layers that support and interconnect these components, offering excellent thermal and electrical properties essential for reliable performance in various electronic devices.</p></p>
