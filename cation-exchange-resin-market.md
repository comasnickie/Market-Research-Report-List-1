<p><strong>The Cation Exchange Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strongly Acidic</li><li>Weak Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cation exchange resins are used to remove positively charged ions from solutions. **Strongly acidic cation exchange resins** contain sulfonic acid groups, making them highly effective across a wide pH range, ideal for water softening and demineralization. **Weak acid cation exchange resins** use carboxylic acid groups, functioning best in neutral to alkaline conditions, often used for dealkalization and selective ion removal. Both types are crucial in industries like water treatment, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals, tailored to specific ion exchange needs.</p></p>
