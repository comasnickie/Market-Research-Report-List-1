<p><strong>The Non-Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Optical Magnetometer market is classified into two main types: absolute and relative magnetometers. Absolute magnetometers measure the total magnetic field strength without reference to other measurements, providing precise absolute values. In contrast, relative magnetometers determine changes in magnetic field strength compared to a reference point, making them useful for detecting variations in magnetic fields. Both types serve vital roles in geophysical surveys, mineral exploration, and military applications, offering distinct advantages depending on the specific measurement requirements.</p></p>
