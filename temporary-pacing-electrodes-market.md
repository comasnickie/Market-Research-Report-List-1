<p><strong>The Temporary Pacing Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Pacing Electrode</li><li>Unipolar Pacing Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temporary pacing electrodes market comprises two main types: bipolar and unipolar pacing electrodes. Bipolar pacing electrodes feature two conductive poles closely spaced, allowing for more localized stimulation and reduced risk of stimulating surrounding tissues. In contrast, unipolar pacing electrodes consist of a single electrode positioned away from the pacing site, creating a wider stimulation area. Each type serves specific clinical needs, impacting surgical procedures, patient conditions, and recovery, thus driving the demand within the temporary pacing electrodes market.</p></p>
