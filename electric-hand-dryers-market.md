<p><p>The electric hand dryers market primarily consists of two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers utilize high-speed air streams to efficiently evaporate moisture from hands, offering quick drying times and reduced energy consumption. In contrast, hot air dryers emit warm air to gradually dry hands, often taking longer and using more electricity. Both types aim to enhance hygiene and convenience in public restrooms, with the choice depending on preferences for speed, energy efficiency, and user experience.</p></p>
