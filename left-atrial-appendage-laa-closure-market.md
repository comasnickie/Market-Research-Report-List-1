<p><strong>The Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epicardial LAA Closure Device</li><li>Endocardial LAA Closure Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) closure market is primarily categorized into two types: epicardial and endocardial LAA closure devices. Epicardial devices are placed on the exterior of the heart to occlude the LAA through a surgical approach, often used during cardiac surgeries. In contrast, endocardial devices are deployed from within the heart via catheterization, providing a less invasive option for LAA closure. Both types aim to reduce stroke risk in patients with atrial fibrillation by preventing blood clots.</p></p>
