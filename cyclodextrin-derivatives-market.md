<p><strong>The Cyclodextrin Derivatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HP-β-CD</li><li>SBE-β-CD</li><li>RM-β-CD</li><li>HP-γ-CD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cyclodextrin derivatives are modified forms of natural cyclodextrins, widely used in pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics for their ability to enhance solubility, stability, and bioavailability of compounds. **HP-β-CD** (Hydroxypropyl-beta-cyclodextrin) is water-soluble and commonly used in drug delivery. **SBE-β-CD** (Sulfobutylether-beta-cyclodextrin) is anionic, offering improved biocompatibility for injectable formulations. **RM-β-CD** (Randomly methylated-beta-cyclodextrin) enhances solubility for hydrophobic drugs. **HP-γ-CD** (Hydroxypropyl-gamma-cyclodextrin) has a larger cavity, suitable for complexing larger molecules. Each derivative caters to specific applications based on solubility, safety, and molecular size requirements.</p></p>
