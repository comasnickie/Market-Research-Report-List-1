<p><p>Ileostomy is a surgical procedure that involves creating an opening in the abdominal wall to allow waste from the small intestine to exit the body, bypassing the colon. This is commonly performed in patients with conditions such as inflammatory bowel disease, colorectal cancer, or severe diverticulitis. The waste is collected in a pouch attached to the skin, providing a solution for individuals who no longer have normal bowel function.</p><p>The Ileostomy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing incidences of gastrointestinal disorders and a growing aging population. Technological advancements in product design, such as improved pouch systems offering better comfort and odor control, are enhancing patient experiences and driving market demand. Additionally, the rising awareness regarding ostomy care and innovation in skin-friendly adhesives contribute to market growth. The expansion of healthcare infrastructure and accessibility in emerging markets further supports the proliferation of ileostomy products and accessories, positioning the market for robust growth in the coming years.</p></p>
