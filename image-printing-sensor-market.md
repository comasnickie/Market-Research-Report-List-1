<p><p>The image printing sensor market encompasses various printing technologies, notably inkjet, flexographic (flexo), and screen printing. Inkjet printing uses droplets of ink to create images, offering high precision and versatility for various materials. Flexo printing employs flexible relief plates for continuous patterns, ideal for packaging and labels due to its speed and efficiency. Screen printing involves forcing ink through a mesh stencil, providing vibrant colors and durability on diverse surfaces. Each method caters to specific applications within the broader printing industry.</p></p>
