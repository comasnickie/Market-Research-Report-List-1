<p><strong>The Automotive HVAC Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condenser</li><li>Evaporator</li><li>Heat Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive HVAC heat exchanger market consists of three primary types: condensers, evaporators, and heat cores. Condensers are responsible for dissipating heat from the refrigerant, converting it from gas to liquid. Evaporators absorb heat from the cabin air, enabling the cooling process by transforming the refrigerant back into gas. Heat cores, often used for heating, transfer engine coolant heat into the cabin. Together, these components ensure efficient temperature control and comfort in vehicles by managing heat exchange effectively.</p></p>
