<p><p>The CMOS digital isolators market is primarily segmented into unidirectional and bidirectional digital isolators. Unidirectional digital isolators allow data transmission in one direction, providing isolation and signal integrity, making them ideal for applications where control signals need to be sent without feedback. In contrast, bidirectional digital isolators enable data flow in both directions, facilitating two-way communication between circuits. This versatility makes bidirectional isolators suitable for more complex systems requiring interactive data exchange, enhancing overall system reliability and performance.</p></p>
