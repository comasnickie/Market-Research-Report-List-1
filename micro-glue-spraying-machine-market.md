<p><strong>The Micro Glue Spraying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Glue Spraying Machine market is primarily divided into two types: contact and contactless. Contact machines directly apply adhesive by physically touching the surface, ensuring precise placement but requiring careful handling to prevent damage. In contrast, contactless machines use air pressure or other methods to spray adhesive without touching the material, allowing for faster application and reduced risk of contamination. Both types cater to different industrial needs, focusing on efficiency, precision, and the desired quality of adhesive bonding.</p></p>
