<p><strong>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV)-1 Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs)</li><li>Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs)</li><li>Entry and Fusion Inhibitors</li><li>Protease Inhibitors (PIs)</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li><li>Coreceptor Antagonists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV-1 therapeutics market comprises various drug classes targeting different stages of the viral life cycle. Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs) inhibit viral RNA transcription into DNA. Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs) block reverse transcriptase through allosteric mechanisms. Entry and Fusion Inhibitors prevent viral entry into host cells. Protease Inhibitors (PIs) disrupt viral protease activity, inhibiting protein processing. Integrase Inhibitors block viral integration into host DNA, while Coreceptor Antagonists inhibit viral entry by targeting host cell receptors.</p></p>
