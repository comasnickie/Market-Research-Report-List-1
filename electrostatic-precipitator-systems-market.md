<p><p>Electrostatic precipitator systems are crucial for air pollution control, categorized mainly into two types: dry and wet electrostatic precipitators. Dry electrostatic precipitators use high-voltage electric fields to charge and collect particulate matter from gas streams, operating effectively in dry conditions. In contrast, wet electrostatic precipitators utilize water or other liquid mediums to capture pollutants, making them suitable for handling moisture-laden gases. Both types are essential for industries aiming to meet environmental regulations and reduce emissions effectively.</p></p>
