<p><ul><li>Non-adjustable Steering Columns</li><li>Manually Adjustable Steering Columns</li><li>Electrically Adjustable Steering Columns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering column systems are categorized into three main types: non-adjustable, manually adjustable, and electrically adjustable steering columns. Non-adjustable steering columns offer a fixed position, enhancing stability but limiting customization. Manually adjustable columns allow drivers to modify the steering wheel's position using a lever or release mechanism, improving comfort and driving posture. Electrically adjustable columns provide electronic controls for seamless adjustments, offering superior convenience and precision, often integrating memory settings for multiple drivers. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
