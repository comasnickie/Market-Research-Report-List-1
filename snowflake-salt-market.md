<p><p>Snowflake salt is categorized into two main market types: granule and lamellate. Granule salt features larger crystal sizes, making it suitable for various culinary applications, including seasoning and garnish. It dissolves quickly, enhancing flavor. In contrast, lamellate salt consists of thin, flat crystals, offering a delicate texture and visual appeal, often used in gourmet cooking and finishing dishes. Both types serve unique niches within the market, catering to different consumer preferences and culinary needs.</p></p>
