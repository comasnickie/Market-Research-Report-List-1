<p><ul><li>Actuators</li><li>Control Element</li><li>Air Treatment Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic components market encompasses three primary types: actuators, control elements, and air treatment components. Actuators convert compressed air into mechanical motion, driving various machinery and tools. Control elements, such as valves and sensors, regulate air flow and pressure, ensuring optimal performance and safety in systems. Air treatment components include filters, regulators, and lubricators, which clean and condition the compressed air to maintain efficiency and prolong equipment life. Together, these components facilitate the effective use of pneumatic systems in diverse applications.</p></p>
