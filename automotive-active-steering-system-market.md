<p><strong>The Automotive Active Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Front Axle Steering</li><li>Active Rear Axle Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active steering system market comprises two primary types: active front axle steering and active rear axle steering. Active front axle steering adjusts the angle of the front wheels in response to vehicle speed and driving conditions, enhancing stability and maneuverability. In contrast, active rear axle steering alters the rear wheel angle to improve cornering performance and reduce turning radius at low speeds. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle handling, safety, and overall driving experience by providing improved responsiveness and control.</p></p>
