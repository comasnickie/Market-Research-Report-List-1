<p><p>The floor insulation material market includes various types such as EPS/XPS, RockWool, PIR/PU, GlassWool, and others. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer excellent thermal resistance. RockWool, made from volcanic rock, provides fire resistance and soundproofing. PIR (Polyisocyanurate) and PU (Polyurethane) offer high thermal efficiency and moisture resistance. GlassWool, made from recycled glass, is lightweight, fire-resistant, and provides sound insulation. Other materials include natural fibers and aerogels, catering to specific needs like sustainability or extreme insulation performance. Each type serves diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial flooring.</p></p>
