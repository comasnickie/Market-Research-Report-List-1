<p><ul><li>Enhancement Type</li><li>Depletion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The N-channel MOSFET transistor market is categorized into two main types: enhancement-type and depletion-type. Enhancement-type MOSFETs are designed to conduct when a positive voltage is applied to the gate, allowing them to efficiently switch current. In contrast, depletion-type MOSFETs are normally on and require a negative gate voltage to turn off. Both types are crucial in various applications, including power management, signal amplification, and digital circuits, catering to different performance and power consumption needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
