<p><p>The LV Circuit Breaker market is segmented into three main types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breakers (ACB). MCBs protect low-capacity circuits from overloads and short circuits, ideal for residential applications. MCCBs handle medium-capacity circuits and offer adjustable settings, suitable for industrial usage. ACBs are designed for high-capacity circuits, often used in large commercial and industrial installations, featuring robust protection and operational reliability under varying load conditions.</p></p>
