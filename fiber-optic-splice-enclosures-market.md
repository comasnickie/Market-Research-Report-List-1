<p><p>Fiber optic splice enclosures are essential components in network installations, providing protection and organization for fiber connections. The market primarily consists of two types: dome type and horizontal type enclosures. Dome type enclosures feature a rounded design, offering superior space for fiber management and accommodating various splice configurations, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Horizontal type enclosures, on the other hand, have a flat design and are typically used in indoor settings, allowing for efficient space utilization and easier access for maintenance. Both types ensure reliable protection and performance in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
