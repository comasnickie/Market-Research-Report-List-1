<p><p>The SSL certification market consists of three main types: Domain Validation (DV), Organization Validation (OV), and Extended Validation (EV). DV certificates verify domain ownership and are easy to obtain, making them suitable for personal websites. OV certificates provide a higher level of trust by validating the organization’s identity, ideal for businesses. EV certificates offer the highest level of security, requiring extensive verification of the organization’s legal status, thus enhancing consumer trust, particularly for e-commerce sites and financial institutions.</p></p>
