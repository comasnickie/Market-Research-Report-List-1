<p><ul><li>Gas Hydraulic Power Unit</li><li>Electric Hydraulic Power Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydraulic Power Unit (HPU) market consists of two primary types: Gas Hydraulic Power Units and Electric Hydraulic Power Units. Gas Hydraulic Power Units operate using compressed gas to generate hydraulic pressure, making them suitable for remote locations and applications where electricity is unavailable. In contrast, Electric Hydraulic Power Units utilize electric motors to power hydraulic pumps, offering advantages in efficiency and control. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, construction, and automotive, providing tailored solutions for specific operational needs.</p></p>
