<p><p>The liquid phytase market is categorized into two main types: acid phytase and alkaline phytase. Acid phytases are effective in lower pH environments, making them suitable for animal feed formulations that promote nutrient absorption in the stomach, enhancing digestibility. In contrast, alkaline phytases perform optimally in higher pH conditions, suitable for digestive systems of ruminants and poultry. Both types aim to improve phosphorus availability from plant-based feed, supporting livestock health and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
