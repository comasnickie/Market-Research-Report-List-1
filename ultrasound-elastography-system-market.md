<p><p>The Ultrasound Elastography System market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Static Elastography (SE)** assesses tissue stiffness through a single image, providing quick insights into lesions.</p><p>2. **Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS)** evaluates tissue response to continuous vibrations, offering real-time elasticity measurements.</p><p>3. **Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli (DETS)** utilizes brief mechanical disturbances to gauge tissue elasticity, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p><p>4. **Other market** encompasses advanced methodologies and hybrid approaches, integrating various techniques for comprehensive tissue characterization.</p></p>
