<p><strong>The Conventional Island Deaerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray Type Deaerator</li><li>Spray Deaerator</li><li>Membrane deaerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional island deaerator market comprises three main types: tray type deaerators, spray deaerators, and membrane deaerators. Tray type deaerators utilize a series of trays to create large surface areas for gas exchange, effectively removing dissolved gases. Spray deaerators introduce water in fine droplets, enhancing contact with steam for efficient gas removal. Membrane deaerators use selective permeable membranes to separate gases from water, offering compact designs and lower operating costs. Each type serves specific industrial applications based on efficiency and space requirements.</p></p>
