<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Grains market comprises two primary types: Black Silicon Carbide Grains and Green Silicon Carbide Grains. Black Silicon Carbide is manufactured from silica sand and petroleum coke, known for its hardness and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for abrasives and refractories. Green Silicon Carbide, produced from high-purity silica and petroleum derivatives, is finer and more pure, ideal for demanding applications such as high-performance ceramics and cutting tools. Both types cater to various industrial needs, enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
