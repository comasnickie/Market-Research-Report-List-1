<p><strong>The Dental Zirconia Blanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Zirconia Blanks</li><li>Multilayer Zirconia Blanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental Zirconia Blanks market is divided into two main types: Monolayer and Multilayer Zirconia Blanks. Monolayer zirconia blanks consist of a single uniform layer, providing consistent mechanical properties and are typically used for single crowns or simple restorations. In contrast, multilayer zirconia blanks feature varying translucency and aesthetics across different layers, making them ideal for complex restorations that require a more natural appearance. Both types are valued for their strength, durability, and exceptional biocompatibility in dental applications.</p></p>
