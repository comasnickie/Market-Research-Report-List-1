<p><p>The DC Gaussmeter market consists of two primary types: 1-axis and 3-axis Gaussmeters. A 1-axis DC Gaussmeter measures magnetic field strength along a single axis, making it suitable for specific applications where directional measurement is sufficient. In contrast, a 3-axis DC Gaussmeter captures magnetic fields across three orthogonal axes simultaneously, providing comprehensive field analysis. This versatility makes the 3-axis version ideal for complex environments where multi-directional magnetic field monitoring is essential, catering to various industrial and research applications.</p></p>
