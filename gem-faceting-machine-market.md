<p><p>The gem faceting machine market is primarily categorized into single axis and dual axis machines. Single axis machines operate on one rotational plane, allowing for simpler designs and efficient gemstone shaping, ideal for beginners and small-scale operations. In contrast, dual axis machines provide enhanced precision and versatility by enabling facets to be cut on two disc axes, accommodating complex gemstone shapes. This advanced functionality attracts professional gem cutters seeking intricate designs and higher-quality finishes, driving distinct market demands within the industry.</p></p>
