<p><p>The myomectomy market is categorized into three main types: Abdominal Myomectomy, Hysteroscopic Myomectomy, and Laparoscopic Myomectomy. Abdominal myomectomy involves an open surgical approach to remove fibroids through an abdominal incision, often used for larger or multiple fibroids. Hysteroscopic myomectomy is a minimally invasive procedure performed via the cervix, ideal for fibroids located within the uterine cavity. Laparoscopic myomectomy uses small incisions and specialized instruments for minimally invasive surgery, offering quicker recovery and reduced scarring. Each type addresses fibroid removal based on individual patient needs and fibroid characteristics.</p></p>
