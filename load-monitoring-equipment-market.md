<p><ul><li>Load Cell</li><li>Indicator and Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The load monitoring equipment market comprises three primary types: load cells, indicators, and controllers. Load cells are sensor devices that convert a force or load into an electrical signal, allowing for accurate weight measurement. Indicators display this data in a readable format, often providing additional functions like data logging or connectivity. Controllers manage and regulate the load measurements, often incorporating features for automation and real-time monitoring. Together, these components enable precise load management in various industrial applications.</p></p>
