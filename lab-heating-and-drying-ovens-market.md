<p><ul><li>Forced Air Circulation Ovens</li><li>Gravity Convection Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab heating and drying ovens are essential equipment in various scientific and industrial applications. Forced air circulation ovens utilize fans to distribute heated air evenly, ensuring uniform temperature throughout the chamber for consistent drying and heating results. In contrast, gravity convection ovens rely on natural convection, where warmer air rises and cooler air descends, creating a gentler heating process. Each type serves specific needs, with forced air ovens often preferred for faster drying, while gravity convection ovens are suitable for delicate materials.</p></p>
