<p><p>The Emergency Evacuation Chair market is categorized into two primary types based on their functionality: descending stairs and ascending stairs. Descending stair chairs are designed to facilitate the safe and efficient evacuation of individuals down stairs during emergencies, utilizing gravity-assisted mechanisms. In contrast, ascending stair chairs are engineered to help users move up stairs, typically for situations where evacuation or access to higher floors is necessary. Both types prioritize safety, ease of use, and mobility for individuals with limited mobility during emergencies.</p></p>
