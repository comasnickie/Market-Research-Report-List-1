<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photo-electric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-beam Sensors</li><li>Retro-reflective Sensors</li><li>Diffuse-reflective Sensors</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo-electric sensor market encompasses several types, including through-beam sensors, retro-reflective sensors, diffuse-reflective sensors, and others. Through-beam sensors consist of a separate transmitter and receiver, ensuring high accuracy over long distances. Retro-reflective sensors utilize a reflector to bounce light back to the receiver, offering a compact solution with reliable performance. Diffuse-reflective sensors detect objects based on scattered light from the target, making them ideal for closer ranges. Other types may include fiber-optic sensors and laser sensors, each serving specific applications.</p></p>
