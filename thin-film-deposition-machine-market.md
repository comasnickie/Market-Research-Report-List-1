<p><p>The thin-film deposition machine market comprises three primary types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) equipment, Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) equipment, and Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) equipment. PVD utilizes physical processes to deposit thin films, primarily for semiconductor and solar applications. CVD involves chemical reactions to create films, valued for its uniformity and conformity on complex surfaces. ALD offers atomic-scale precision, making it ideal for advanced nanoscale devices, enabling intricate coatings with exceptional thickness control and uniformity. Each type serves specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
