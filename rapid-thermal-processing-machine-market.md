<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) machine market is primarily categorized into lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP utilizes intense light sources, typically halogen lamps, to quickly heat semiconductor wafers, enabling precise temperature control and uniformity. In contrast, laser-based RTP employs focused laser beams to provide targeted heating, offering greater precision and faster processing times for intricate patterns. Both technologies are essential for enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of semiconductor fabrication processes in advanced manufacturing settings.</p></p>
