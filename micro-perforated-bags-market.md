<p><p>Micro perforated bags are typically categorized based on the material used in their production. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) provides clarity and moisture resistance, ideal for food packaging. PA (Polyamide) is known for its excellent barrier properties, often used for vacuum-sealed products. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) offers flexibility and seal integrity, commonly used in various packaging applications. Other materials may include biodegradable options, catering to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
