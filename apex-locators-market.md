<p><p>Apex locators are devices used in endodontics to determine the location of the root canal's apex. The market for apex locators can be categorized into two primary types: alarm type and digital readout types. Alarm type apex locators provide auditory or visual signals when the file reaches the apical area, aiding dentists in real-time decision-making. Digital readout types display precise measurements on a screen, offering detailed information about the file's position and the root canal's anatomy, improving accuracy during procedures.</p></p>
