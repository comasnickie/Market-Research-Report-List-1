<p><p>The Advanced Foam Wound Dressings market features two primary types: Adhesive and Non-Adhesive Foam Dressings. Adhesive Foam Dressings have an integral adhesive layer that allows for easy application and secure attachment to the skin, promoting moisture retention and healing. Conversely, Non-Adhesive Foam Dressings lack built-in adhesive, requiring supplemental fixation with medical tape or wraps. Both types are designed to absorb exudate, provide cushioning, and create a conducive environment for wound healing, making them essential in modern wound care management.</p></p>
