<p><strong>The PP Plastic Closure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Moulding</li><li>Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP plastic closure market primarily utilizes two manufacturing techniques: compression molding and injection molding. Compression molding involves shaping heated polypropylene material in a mold under pressure, allowing for efficient mass production of closures with consistent quality. In contrast, injection molding injects molten polypropylene into a mold, providing intricate designs and fast production cycles. Both methods are widely used in packaging, particularly for beverages and food products, due to their durability, cost-effectiveness, and ability to create lightweight, versatile closures.</p></p>
