<p><ul><li>Natural Cheese</li><li>Process Cheese</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cow cheese market is primarily divided into two types: natural cheese and processed cheese. Natural cheese is made directly from milk through fermentation, retaining its original flavors and textures. It includes varieties like cheddar, mozzarella, and gouda. On the other hand, processed cheese is engineered from natural cheese, often combined with emulsifiers and other ingredients, resulting in a product that is uniform, meltable, and shelf-stable. Both cater to different consumer preferences and culinary applications in diverse markets.</p></p>
