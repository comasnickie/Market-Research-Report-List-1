<p><strong>The Laminated Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure Laminate (HPL)</li><li>Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated panels market includes High Pressure Laminate (HPL) and Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL) types. HPL is produced by bonding layers of resin-impregnated paper under high heat and pressure, resulting in durable, scratch-resistant surfaces often used in countertops and furniture. Conversely, CPL involves continuous processing, creating thinner, flexible laminates suitable for wall panels and decorative applications. Both types offer diverse design options and applications, catering to various aesthetic and functional needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
