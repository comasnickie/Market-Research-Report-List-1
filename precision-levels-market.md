<p><ul><li>Spirit Level</li><li>Laser Level</li><li>Optical Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision levels, including spirit levels, laser levels, and optical levels, are essential tools used in construction, surveying, and engineering. Spirit levels utilize a bubble vial to indicate horizontal or vertical alignment, making them simple and effective for basic tasks. Laser levels project clear, straight lines over long distances, offering higher accuracy for large projects. Optical levels use a telescope and graduated staff for precise measurements, ideal for surveying applications. Each type serves specific needs based on accuracy, convenience, and application scope.</p></p>
