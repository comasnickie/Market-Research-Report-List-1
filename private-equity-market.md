<p><p>Private equity markets can be categorized into three main types: Trust Funds, Contractual Funds, and Capital Commitment Markets. Trust Funds pool investments from multiple sources, providing a diversified portfolio managed by professionals. Contractual Funds involve agreements between investors and fund managers, outlining specific investment strategies and returns. Capital Commitment Markets focus on commitments made by investors to provide funds for a defined duration, allowing managers to invest in opportunities as they arise. Each type offers unique benefits and investment structures.</p></p>
