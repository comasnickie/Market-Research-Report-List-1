<p><p>The Hazmat labels market is primarily divided into two types: DOT (Department of Transportation) Hazmat labels and EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) Hazmat labels. DOT Hazmat labels are designed to indicate hazardous materials during transportation, ensuring safety and compliance with federal regulations. Conversely, EPA Hazmat labels focus on environmental hazards, aiding in the proper handling and disposal of dangerous substances to protect public health and ecosystems. Both types are crucial for industries managing hazardous materials to maintain safety and regulatory adherence.</p></p>
