<p><p>The industrial nitric acid market is primarily divided into two types: dilute nitric acid and concentrated nitric acid. Dilute nitric acid, typically containing less than 70% nitric acid, is used in applications where a lower acidity is sufficient, such as in fertilizers and certain chemical processes. In contrast, concentrated nitric acid, with a concentration of 70% or higher, is utilized in more demanding applications like explosives manufacturing, metal processing, and specialty chemicals, due to its stronger oxidative properties.</p></p>
