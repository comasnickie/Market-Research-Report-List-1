<p><p>The Polyethylene Coatings market features two primary types: two-component and one-component polyurethane coatings. Two-component polyurethane coatings require mixing a resin with a hardener prior to application, offering exceptional durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, one-component polyurethane coatings are ready-to-use and cure upon exposure to moisture, providing convenience and ease of application, typically favored for residential and less demanding environments. Both types enhance protection and longevity for various surfaces in multiple industries.</p></p>
