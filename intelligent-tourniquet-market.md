<p><p>The Intelligent Tourniquet market is categorized into Single Port and Dual Port types. Single Port tourniquets feature one access point for the inflating mechanism, making them simpler and easier to apply. In contrast, Dual Port tourniquets have two access points, allowing for more precise pressure control and simultaneous monitoring of multiple limbs. The choice between these types often depends on the specific medical needs during emergency situations, with the Dual Port option providing enhanced functionality for complex injuries.</p></p>
