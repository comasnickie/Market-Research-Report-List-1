<p><p>The dental acrylic market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Heat cure acrylic resin is known for its durability and high strength, commonly used in denture bases and prosthetics. Self-curing acrylic resin hardens chemically at room temperature, offering convenience and ease of use in chairside applications. Cold cure acrylic resin also cures at room temperature but is ideal for quick repairs. Light-cured acrylic resin offers rapid setting times under UV light, enhancing precision in aesthetic dental procedures.</p></p>
