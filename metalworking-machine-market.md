<p><strong>The Metalworking Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metalforming</li><li>Metalcutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metalworking machine market is divided into two main types: metal forming and metal cutting. Metal forming encompasses processes that reshape metal without removing material, including techniques like stamping, forging, and bending. In contrast, metal cutting involves removing material to achieve desired shapes and sizes, typically using tools like lathes, milling machines, and saws. Both segments are critical for various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, as they facilitate the production of precise metal components.</p></p>
