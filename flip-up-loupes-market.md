<p><p>The Flip-Up Loupes market consists of two primary types: Galilean Lens and Prismatic Lens. Galilean Loupes utilize a simple and lightweight design, offering a wide field of view with less distortion. They are often preferred for general dental and surgical applications. In contrast, Prismatic Loupes provide higher magnification and improved depth perception, suitable for intricate procedures. Both types cater to healthcare professionals, enhancing visibility and precision during tasks, ultimately improving patient outcomes and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
