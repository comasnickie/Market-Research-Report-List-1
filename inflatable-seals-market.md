<p><p>The inflatable seals market is categorized based on material types, each offering unique properties. Silicone seals are known for their flexibility and temperature resistance. SBR/NBR (Styrene-Butadiene/Rubber) provides good abrasion resistance. EPDM seals excel in weather and ozone resistance, making them suitable for outdoor applications. FKM (Fluoroelastomer) offers excellent chemical resistance. Neoprene seals balance oil resistance and flexibility. Other materials may include polyurethane and thermoplastic elastomers, catering to specific industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
