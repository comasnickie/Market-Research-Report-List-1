<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis (HD)</li><li>Peritoneal dialysis (PD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home dialysis systems market comprises two primary types: Hemodialysis (HD) and Peritoneal Dialysis (PD). HD involves using a machine to filter blood, typically requiring patients to connect to the system several times a week. It can be done at home or in a clinic. In contrast, PD utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter waste and requires less frequent interventions, allowing for more flexibility and independence. Both methods aim to improve patients' quality of life.</p></p>
