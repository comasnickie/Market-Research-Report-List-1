<p><p>The conductive tapes market includes two primary types: double coated and single coated tapes. Double coated tapes feature adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure bonding of conductive materials, often used in applications like shielding and grounding. Single coated tapes have adhesive on only one side, making them ideal for attaching conductive materials to surfaces while providing effective electrical connectivity. Both types are essential in electronics, telecommunications, and automotive industries, offering solutions for electromagnetic interference and static discharge management.</p></p>
