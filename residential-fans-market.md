<p><p>The residential fan market is divided into two main types: AC and DC residential fans. AC residential fans operate on alternating current, providing efficient cooling and robust performance, making them a popular choice in many households. In contrast, DC residential fans utilize direct current, which offers energy efficiency and often quieter operation. DC fans typically consume less power while providing similar airflow, making them suitable for eco-conscious consumers. Both types cater to diverse preferences and energy-saving needs in residential settings.</p></p>
