<p><strong>The Gooseneck Trailer Hitches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under-bed Gooseneck</li><li>Over/Above-bed Gooseneck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gooseneck trailer hitches are heavy-duty towing systems that connect trailers to vehicles via a ball mounted in the truck bed. **Under-bed gooseneck hitches** are installed beneath the truck bed, offering a clean, flush appearance and preserving bed space. **Over/above-bed gooseneck hitches** sit on top of the bed, providing easier installation and removal but occupying bed space. Both types are designed for high towing capacity, with under-bed hitches favored for aesthetics and over-bed for convenience and versatility.</p></p>
