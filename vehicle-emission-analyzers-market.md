<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle emission analyzers market includes several technologies: Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers measure gas concentrations by detecting infrared light absorption, while Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) quantify hydrocarbons through ionization in a flame. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) focus on nitrogen oxides by measuring light emitted during reactions. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect exhaust samples for analysis, ensuring consistent measurement conditions. Other types encompass advanced and emerging technologies that enhance the accuracy and efficiency of vehicle emission testing.</p></p>
