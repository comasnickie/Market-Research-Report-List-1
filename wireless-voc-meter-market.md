<p><strong>The Wireless VOC Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless VOC Meter market comprises various technologies, primarily PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID sensors detect volatile organic compounds by using ultraviolet light to ionize molecules, offering high sensitivity to low concentrations. Conversely, MOS sensors rely on changes in resistance caused by adsorbed gases, providing a cost-effective and compact option for detecting VOCs. Both types are integral in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and indoor air quality management, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
