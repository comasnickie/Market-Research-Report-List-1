<p><p>Agricultural pheromones are signaling chemicals produced by insects that play a crucial role in communication, particularly in mating and navigation. Additionally, the growing focus on integrated pest management (IPM) strategies, which combine biological, cultural, and chemical practices, further propels the adoption of pheromones.
