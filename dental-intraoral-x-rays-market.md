<p><strong>The Dental Intraoral X-Rays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental intraoral X-ray market comprises several types, each serving specific diagnostic purposes. Bitewing X-rays capture the upper and lower teeth in one image, helping identify cavities between teeth and assess bone levels. Periapical X-rays focus on individual teeth, revealing roots and surrounding bone for comprehensive analysis of conditions like infections or abscesses. Additionally, other types may include occlusal X-rays, which show the full arch of teeth in the jaw, assisting in detecting cysts or abnormalities. Each type enhances diagnostic accuracy in dentistry.</p></p>
