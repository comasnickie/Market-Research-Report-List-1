<p><strong>The Dash Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Dash Cameras</li><li>Multi-Channel Dash Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dash cameras are vehicle-mounted devices that record video while driving. Single-channel dash cameras feature one lens, capturing footage from the front of the vehicle, ideal for basic recording needs. Multi-channel dash cameras have multiple lenses (e.g., dual or triple), recording from the front, rear, and sometimes interior, offering comprehensive coverage for enhanced security and evidence collection. Both types cater to different user requirements, with single-channel being cost-effective and multi-channel providing advanced monitoring for commercial or high-security applications.</p></p>
