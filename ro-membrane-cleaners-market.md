<p><ul><li>Acid Membrane Cleaners</li><li>Alkaline Membrane Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO membrane cleaners market is divided into two primary types: acid membrane cleaners and alkaline membrane cleaners. Acid membrane cleaners are designed to remove scale and inorganic deposits from membranes, effectively dissolving mineral buildups such as calcium and magnesium. In contrast, alkaline membrane cleaners target organic foulants like fats, oils, and biological materials, breaking them down to restore membrane performance. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of reverse osmosis systems in various industries.</p></p>
