<p><strong>The Home and Property Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Basic Form</li><li>Broad Form</li><li>Special Form</li><li>Tenant's Form</li><li>Comprehensive Form</li><li>Condo Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home and property insurance market types include:</p><p>1. **Basic Form**: Covers named perils like fire and theft.</p><p>2. **Broad Form**: Extends coverage to additional risks, including certain damages to the structure.</p><p>3. **Special Form**: Offers comprehensive protection against all perils, except those explicitly excluded.</p><p>4. **Tenant's Form**: Designed for renters, covering personal property and liability.</p><p>5. **Comprehensive Form**: Provides extensive coverage for both property and liability.</p><p>6. **Condo Form**: Tailored for condo owners, covering personal property and shared building elements.</p></p>
