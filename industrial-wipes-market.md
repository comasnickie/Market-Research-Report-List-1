<p><p>The industrial wipes market comprises three main types: cellulose, poly/cellulose blend, and woodpulp/polyester. Cellulose wipes are highly absorbent and biodegradable, making them ideal for tasks that require eco-friendly options. Poly/cellulose blends offer durability and strength, suitable for heavy-duty applications while retaining some biodegradability. Woodpulp/polyester wipes combine the absorbent properties of woodpulp with the toughness of polyester, providing effective cleaning solutions for industrial use. Together, these types cater to diverse cleaning and maintenance needs across various industries.</p></p>
