<p><p>Linear Alkylbenzene Sulfonate (LAS) is a key surfactant used in detergents and cleaning products. The market is divided into Hard LAS and Soft LAS based on their chemical structure and performance. Hard LAS has a longer alkyl chain, making it more effective in hard water but less biodegradable. Soft LAS has a shorter alkyl chain, offering better biodegradability and environmental compatibility, though it may be less effective in hard water. Both types cater to different industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
