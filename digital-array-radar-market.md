<p><ul><li>Active Digital Array Radar</li><li>Passive Digital Array Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Array Radar market is categorized into Active Digital Array Radar (ADAR) and Passive Digital Array Radar (PDAR). ADAR systems utilize active elements that transmit and receive signals, allowing for precise targeting and superior resolution, making them suitable for applications like air traffic control and military surveillance. In contrast, PDAR systems rely on external sources for signal illumination, offering cost-effectiveness and lower power requirements, primarily used in passive detection scenarios, such as maritime and ground monitoring.</p></p>
