<p><p>The disposable gloves market consists of three main types: latex, vinyl, and nitrile. Latex gloves are known for their elasticity and comfort, making them popular in medical settings, but they can cause allergic reactions in some users. Vinyl gloves are a cost-effective alternative, suitable for low-risk tasks, although they offer less protection than latex or nitrile. Nitrile gloves are durable, puncture-resistant, and safe for those with latex allergies, making them ideal for various healthcare and industrial applications.</p></p>
